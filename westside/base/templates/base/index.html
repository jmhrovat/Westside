OUR MISSION

The purpose of Westside Christian School is threefold. First, our primary goal is to be evangelical. Our enrollment is open to nonbelievers with the hope of introducing boys and girls or even moms and dads to Jesus Christ. Second, we desire to equip the saints by giving them a Christian view of God and the world based on the authoritative and inerrant Word of God. Third, we desire an outstanding education for students Pre-K through sixth grade!
